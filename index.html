Kyrie's career

Kyrie irving is easily one of the best players in the NBA at the moment. Easily scoring 34 points against the LA Lakers. In Irving's whole career he scored an average of 11,712 total points. Also while playing for the Boston Celtics he easily carried his team onto the NBA finals. While playing against his former team mate Lebron James he was swiftly able to score on him during the Nets vs lakers game.

Important dates

  • June 23,2011 Kyrie Irving was drafted into the NBA.
  • June of 2011 Beginning his career with the Cleveland Calvaliers.
  • In 2016 played for the USA in the FIBA in the Olympics
  • October 25,2011 Kyrie won his first NBA Championship.
  • 2017-2019 Kyrie Played for the Boston Celtics.
  • 2019-Present Signed with a free agent for the Brooklyn Nets.
